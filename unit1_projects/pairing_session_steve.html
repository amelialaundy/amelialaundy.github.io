Reflection:

Amelia:
I really enjoyed this first pairing session with Steve, we started by having a brief chat before the guided session started. I think this really helped as we could gauge where each other were, this was especially helpful for me to know where Steve was as he has only just started the course and so playing catch up. This meant I could check where he had got to, what he may or may not have covered yet etc.

During the remote wireframing we communicated well, and considered different factors of the design. The same goes for the coding session, we worked well with Steve as the navigator-writing the code, and myself driving.

I don't think anything didn't work, of course we had a few issues with how do we write this? or where should this go? but in terms of the actual pairing experience I think it went smoothly.
