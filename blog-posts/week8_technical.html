Recursion


What does recursion mean?

The word recursion comes from the word recur, (to repeat, occur again, happen again). In relation to programming it refers to steps in a procedure that invoke the procedure itself. This can often end with an infinite loop unless there is an instruction to break if something happens, for example an if / else statement or a when statement.
